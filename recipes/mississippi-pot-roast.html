Mississippi Pot Roast

Mississippi Pot Roast

Description

This internet-famous variation on a classic dish is loaded with umami and easy to make in a slow cooker. The hardest part is waiting for it to cook!

Ingredients

  • 4 lbs chuck roast
  • Ranch dip packet
  • Brown gravy packet
  • 1/2 cup (1 stick) unsalted butter
  • 10+ peperoncini peppers
  • 1/4 cup peperoncini juice

Directions

  1. Add the roast to the slow cooker. Optionally, brown the roast in a skillet first.
  2. Add the ranch and gravy packets on top of the roast.
  3. Add the butter, peppers and juice.
  4. Cover and cook on low for 8-10 hours or high for 6-7 hours. Do not open the lip while the roast is cooking.
  5. Shred with two forks, removing any large pieces of fat.
